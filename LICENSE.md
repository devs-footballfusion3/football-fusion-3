## 🐛 Common Issues and Fixes

### ❌ Script GUI doesn't appear after executing
**Fix:** Ensure you are fully loaded into an active Football Fusion 3 match — not in the lobby or on the team selection screen. Re-inject your executor and execute the script again once the field is fully loaded.

### ❌ Ball Magnet isn't pulling the ball
**Fix:** The magnet range has a configurable radius in the GUI. Open the **Magnet** tab and increase the pull radius. Also confirm you're playing as a receiver or the ball carrier role — the magnet only activates for the correct player position.

### ❌ QB Aimbot throws in the wrong direction
**Fix:** This typically happens when multiple receivers are on screen and the targeting system locks onto the wrong one. Use the **Target Lock** toggle in the QB tab to manually select your primary receiver before the snap.

### ❌ Speed Boost causes the character to fly off the map
**Fix:** The multiplier is set too high. Open the Speed tab and reduce the multiplier to 1.5x or lower. Values above 3.0x can cause physics conflicts on certain field zones, especially near end zones.

### ❌ No-Clip stops working mid-play
**Fix:** No-clip is temporarily disabled by the game's collision checks during tackle animations. Toggle it off and back on to re-enable it after a tackle sequence completes.

### ❌ Executor injects but script throws a "nil value" error
**Fix:** The game has likely updated since the script version you're using was released. Check the repository's Releases page for the latest version — nil errors are always resolved in the next patch update within 24 hours.

### ❌ Auto Snap triggers too early or too late
**Fix:** Auto Snap timing can be adjusted in the GUI's **Play Settings** tab. Increase the snap delay value if it's snapping before your receivers complete their routes. The default value of 1.2 seconds works for most standard plays.
